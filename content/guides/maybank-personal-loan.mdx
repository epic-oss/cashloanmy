### Who Gets Approved Easily

- Maybank salary account holders (3+ months)
- Customers with existing Maybank loans paid on time
- Maybank credit card holders with good record
- Professionals (doctors, lawyers, engineers)
- Government servants

### Who May Face Difficulty

- New customers with no Maybank relationship
- Self-employed with inconsistent income
- Those with recent CCRIS late payments
- High DSR (above 60%)
- Multiple recent loan applications

## Documents Required

**For Employed Applicants:**
- MyKad (IC)
- Latest 3 months pay slips
- Latest 3 months bank statements (not needed if Maybank salary account)
- Employment confirmation letter
- EPF statement

**For Self-Employed:**
- MyKad (IC)
- SSM business registration
- Latest 6 months bank statements
- Latest 2 years Form B (tax returns)
- Business profile

## How to Apply

### Option 1: MAE App (Fastest)

1. Download MAE by Maybank app
2. Log in or register
3. Go to "Apply" section
4. Select Personal Loan/Financing
5. Fill in details and upload documents
6. Submit application
7. Receive decision (often within 24-48 hours)
8. E-sign agreement if approved
9. Funds disbursed to your account

### Option 2: Maybank2u Website

1. Log in to www.maybank2u.com.my
2. Navigate to Apply > Loans
3. Select Personal Loan
4. Complete application form
5. Upload required documents
6. Submit and wait for processing

### Option 3: Branch Visit

1. Find nearest Maybank branch
2. Bring all required documents
3. Speak to personal banker
4. Fill application form
5. Wait 3-7 days for decision

<Callout type="info" title="Best Option">
MAE app is fastest — existing Maybank customers often get approval within 1-2 days. New customers should visit branch for better guidance.
</Callout>

## Tips to Get Approved

### Before Applying

1. **Credit salary to Maybank** — Even 3 months helps significantly
2. **Check your CCRIS** — Fix any issues first
3. **Calculate your DSR** — Make sure you're under 60%
4. **Clear small debts** — Reduces DSR and shows responsibility
5. **Don't apply elsewhere first** — Maybank sees all applications

### During Application

1. **Apply via MAE** — Faster than branch for existing customers
2. **Be accurate** — Wrong info causes delays
3. **Upload clear documents** — Blurry photos get rejected
4. **Apply for realistic amount** — Lower amount = higher approval chance
5. **Choose longer tenure** — Lower monthly = easier DSR qualification

### If Rejected

1. **Ask for reason** — Call to understand why
2. **Wait 3-6 months** — Don't immediately apply elsewhere
3. **Fix the issue** — Whether it's DSR, credit, or documents
4. **Build relationship** — Use Maybank more actively
5. **Try again** — Or consider alternatives

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does Maybank personal loan approval take?
For existing customers with salary account: 1-2 working days. For new customers: 3-7 working days.

### What is minimum salary for Maybank personal loan?
Standard requirement is RM2,500/month. Some promotions accept RM2,000/month.

### Can I apply Maybank loan online?
Yes, via MAE app or Maybank2u website. Online is faster for existing customers.

### Is Maybank personal loan rate fixed or variable?
Fixed rate. Your monthly payment stays the same throughout the tenure.

### Can I pay off Maybank loan early?
Yes, early settlement is allowed. You'll receive rebate (ibra') on unearned profit for Islamic financing.

### How to increase Maybank personal loan approval chances?
Credit your salary to Maybank, maintain good account balance, have clean CCRIS, and keep DSR below 60%.
