## Eligibility Requirements

### Who Can Apply

- Malaysian citizens aged 18-60
- Minimum income RM1,500/month
- Government servants, GLC, or private sector employees
- Self-employed with business registration
- Pensioners (with pension slip)

### Documents Required

**For Government Servants:**
- MyKad (IC)
- Latest 3 months salary slip
- Employment confirmation letter
- Latest EPF statement
- Bank statements (if salary not auto-deducted)

**For Private Sector:**
- MyKad (IC)
- Latest 3 months salary slip
- Latest 3 months bank statements
- Employment confirmation letter
- EPF statement

**For Self-Employed:**
- MyKad (IC)
- SSM registration (Form 9 & 49)
- Latest 6 months bank statements
- Business income proof
- Latest income tax return

<Callout type="tip" title="Speed Tip">
Government servants with salary deduction (potongan gaji) get fastest approval — sometimes within 2-3 days.
</Callout>

## How to Apply

### Option 1: Branch Visit (Recommended)

1. Find nearest Bank Rakyat branch
2. Bring all required documents
3. Fill application form
4. Submit and wait for processing
5. Sign agreement if approved
6. Receive funds (to Bank Rakyat account or cheque)

### Option 2: Online Pre-Application

1. Visit www.bankrakyat.com.my
2. Fill online enquiry form
3. Bank will contact you
4. Still need to visit branch to complete

### Application Tips

1. **Apply at your nearest branch** — Avoid applying at branches far from your workplace/home
2. **Bring complete documents** — Missing docs = delays
3. **Go early** — Less queue in the morning
4. **Know your DSR** — Calculate beforehand to know if you qualify

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Berapa lama proses pinjaman Bank Rakyat?
Kebiasaannya 3-7 hari bekerja. Penjawat awam dengan potongan gaji boleh dapat lebih cepat (2-3 hari).

### What is the maximum loan for Bank Rakyat?
Up to RM200,000 depending on your income and DSR. Government servants with long service may qualify for higher amounts.

### Can private sector apply for Bank Rakyat personal loan?
Yes, but rates are slightly higher than for government servants. You need stable employment and clean credit record.

### Is Bank Rakyat loan halal?
Yes, Bank Rakyat personal financing is Shariah compliant (Pembiayaan Peribadi-i) using Islamic financing principles.

### Can I pay off Bank Rakyat loan early?
Yes, early settlement is allowed. Contact your branch for the settlement amount which may include ibra' (rebate) on unearned profit.

### What is the minimum salary for Bank Rakyat personal loan?
Minimum RM1,500 per month gross salary.
