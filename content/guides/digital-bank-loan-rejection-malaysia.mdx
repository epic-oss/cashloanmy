## Why Digital Banks Reject You

### 1. Insufficient Digital Footprint
Digital banks use **app behavior**, not just CCRIS:
- How often you use the app
- Transaction patterns
- Wallet balance history
- E-commerce activity

**Example:** GXBank user who never transacts = likely rejected even with good CCRIS.

### 2. Tightened Risk Models (2025/2026)
After initial launch hype, digital banks reduced approval rates:
- GXBank: Now requires 6+ months active usage
- Boost: Minimum RM500/month in transactions
- Algorithm changes favor long-term users

### 3. Still Check CCRIS
They're more lenient than banks, but:
- Multiple recent rejections = red flag
- Active defaults = automatic no
- DSR still matters

### 4. Low Initial Limits
Even if approved:
- GXBank: RM1,000-5,000 initially
- Boost: RM500-3,000 for new users
- Need usage history to increase

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Misconception">
"Digital bank = easy approval" was true in 2024. Not anymore. They're becoming as selective as traditional banks.
</Callout>

## Should You Appeal Digital Bank Rejection?

**Usually pointless.** Digital banks use algorithms. No human review.

**Instead:**
- Wait 3-6 months
- Build app usage
- Try different digital bank

## GXBank Specific Issues

Common rejection reasons:
- New account (less than 6 months)
- Low transaction volume
- No salary crediting
- Existing high DSR

**Workaround:** Use GXSave (savings), credit salary if possible, use for bills.

## Boost Bank Specific Issues

Common rejection reasons:
- Boost wallet rarely used
- No e-commerce transactions
- Don't use Boost for payments
- Limited reload history

**Workaround:** Active Boost usage for 3-6 months before applying.

## What This Means for 2026

Digital banks are **NOT the easy approval option** anymore.

**Reality:**
- They're becoming like traditional banks
- App usage = new form of "employment verification"
- Best for existing active users

**If you need money urgently:** Skip digital banks, go straight to licensed money lenders or AEON.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why did GXBank reject me with good credit?
Likely insufficient GXBank app usage. They prioritize active users over CCRIS scores.

### Can I apply to multiple digital banks?
Yes, but each creates CCRIS inquiry. Better to build usage on one platform first.

### Which digital bank is easiest in 2026?
AEON Bank (if you're already a member). Otherwise, all require significant app history.

### How long after rejection should I wait?
3-6 months. Use that time to build transaction history in the app.

### Are digital banks better than traditional banks?
For loans? Not really anymore. For savings/features? Yes. For loan approval? About the same.
