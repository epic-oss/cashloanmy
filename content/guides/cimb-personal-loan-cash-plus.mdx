CIMB offers two main personal financing options: regular Personal Loan and Cash Plus. This guide covers both products, who they're best for, and how to maximize your approval chances.

<Callout type="info" title="Cash Plus vs Personal Loan">
Cash Plus is CIMB's quick financing for existing customers. Regular Personal Loan is available to everyone. Existing CIMB customers should check Cash Plus first — it's faster and often easier to get.
</Callout>

## CIMB Personal Loan Overview

## Documents Required

### For Personal Loan

**Employed:**
- MyKad (IC)
- Latest 3 months pay slips
- Latest 3 months bank statements
- Employment letter (if less than 1 year)
- EPF statement

**Self-Employed:**
- MyKad (IC)
- SSM registration
- Latest 6 months bank statements
- Business income proof
- Latest 2 years tax returns

### For Cash Plus

- Usually no additional documents needed
- Pre-approved based on your CIMB account data
- May require IC verification only

## How to Apply

### CIMB Personal Loan

**Option 1: Online**
1. Visit www.cimb.com.my
2. Navigate to Personal Loan
3. Fill online application
4. Upload documents
5. Wait for approval call
6. Sign agreement (e-signing or branch)
7. Receive funds

**Option 2: Branch**
1. Visit nearest CIMB branch
2. Bring all documents
3. Fill application form
4. Wait for processing
5. Sign and receive funds

### CIMB Cash Plus

**Via CIMB OCTO App (Fastest)**
1. Open CIMB OCTO app
2. Look for Cash Plus offer (if eligible)
3. Select amount and tenure
4. Confirm application
5. Receive funds within hours

**Via CIMB Clicks**
1. Log in to CIMB Clicks
2. Check for pre-approved offers
3. Apply directly online
4. Instant or same-day approval

<Callout type="info" title="Don't See Cash Plus Offer?">
If Cash Plus doesn't appear in your CIMB app, you may not be pre-qualified yet. Build your relationship — credit salary regularly, maintain healthy balance, use CIMB services — and check again in 1-2 months.
</Callout>

## Who Should Choose CIMB?

### Choose Personal Loan If:
- You need more than RM100,000
- You want longer tenure (6-7 years)
- You're not an existing CIMB customer
- You prefer lower monthly payments

### Choose Cash Plus If:
- You're an existing CIMB customer
- You need money quickly (same day)
- You need smaller amount (under RM50,000)
- You want minimal documentation hassle

## Tips to Improve Approval Chances

1. **Credit salary to CIMB** — Biggest factor for Cash Plus eligibility
2. **Maintain good account balance** — Shows financial stability
3. **Use CIMB credit card responsibly** — Builds internal credit score
4. **Keep CCRIS clean** — No late payments for 12 months
5. **Lower your DSR** — Pay off small debts before applying
6. **Apply for realistic amount** — Don't max out, leave buffer

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How fast is CIMB Cash Plus approval?
For pre-approved customers, approval can be instant to same day. Funds typically disbursed within 24 hours.

### Can I get CIMB personal loan with RM2,000 salary?
Yes, RM2,000 is the minimum requirement. Your loan amount will depend on your DSR (existing commitments vs income).

### What credit score do I need for CIMB loan?
CIMB doesn't publish a minimum score, but clean CCRIS (no late payments in 12 months) significantly helps. Cash Plus is more flexible if you have good CIMB banking behavior.

### Is CIMB Cash Plus the same as personal loan?
Similar but different. Cash Plus is a quick financing facility for existing customers with faster approval. Personal Loan is a standard product with higher limits and longer tenure.

### Can self-employed apply for CIMB loan?
Yes, but you need proper documentation: SSM, bank statements, tax returns. Self-employed typically get higher rates than salaried employees.

### How to check if I have Cash Plus offer?
Log in to CIMB OCTO app or CIMB Clicks. Pre-approved offers appear on your dashboard if you're eligible.
