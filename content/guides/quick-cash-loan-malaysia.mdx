## Same-Day Options — Visit Required

For amounts above RM5,000 or if loan apps aren't available to you, same-day options require an in-person visit.

### Licensed Money Lenders

**What they are:** Companies licensed by KPKT (Ministry of Housing) to provide personal loans. Legal, regulated, but more expensive than banks.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Amount | RM1,000 - RM50,000+ |
| Rate | 12-18% p.a. (legal maximum) |
| Speed | Same day to 3 days |
| Income needed | RM1,500+/month |
| CCRIS check | Yes, but more lenient than banks |

**Same-day approval process:**
1. Call ahead: "I have all documents ready. Can I get approval today?"
2. Visit their office (they MUST have a physical office)
3. Submit application with documents
4. Wait for assessment (1-4 hours)
5. If approved, receive cash or bank transfer same day

**Documents to bring (originals AND copies):**
- MyKad (IC)
- Latest 3 months pay slips
- Latest 3 months bank statements
- Employment confirmation letter
- Utility bill (proof of address)

<Callout type="tip" title="Speed Tip">
Call before visiting. Ask specifically: "If I come with all documents now, can I get cash today?" This saves you a wasted trip to slow-processing lenders.
</Callout>

**How to verify a lender is licensed:**
1. Check the KPKT website (www.kpkt.gov.my) for the licensed list
2. They MUST have a physical office you can visit
3. They will NEVER ask for payment before your loan is approved
4. Interest rate will be clearly stated in the contract

### Ar-Rahnu / Pawn Shops — No Credit Check

**What it is:** Cash for your valuables. No income check, no CCRIS check, no questions.

| Item | Where to Go | You Get | Repayment |
|------|-------------|---------|-----------|
| Gold/jewelry | Ar-Rahnu (Bank Rakyat, Agrobank), pawn shops | 60-70% of value | 6 months |
| Electronics | Pawn shops | 30-50% of value | 3-6 months |

**Process:**
1. Bring your valuables
2. They assess the value
3. Get cash immediately
4. Repay within the term to get your items back
5. If you can't repay, they keep the items (no further debt)

**Best for:** Anyone with gold or valuables who needs cash immediately regardless of credit status.

## Scams Targeting Urgent Borrowers

When you're desperate, scammers know you'll take risks. Here's what to watch for:

### The "Processing Fee" Scam
- "Approved! Just pay RM500 processing fee to release the funds"
- **Reality:** Legitimate lenders deduct fees from disbursement. They NEVER ask for money upfront.

### The "Guaranteed Approval" Scam
- "100% approval regardless of CCRIS!"
- **Reality:** No legitimate lender approves everyone. If they promise this, they'll take your money and disappear.

### The WhatsApp/Telegram Lender
- No office, no website, just a phone number
- **Reality:** They'll collect your IC copy, then steal your identity or become loan sharks.

### The "Agent" Middleman
- Claims to have connections at banks
- Charges fee to "process" your application
- **Reality:** Banks don't work with random agents. This is a scam.

### The Fake Bank Website
- Website looks like a real bank
- Asks you to fill in personal details
- **Reality:** Phishing site collecting your data. Always verify bank URLs.

<Callout type="danger" title="Golden Rule">
If someone promises easy money AND asks you to pay first, it's a scam. 100% of the time. No exceptions. Report to PDRM: 03-2610 1222.
</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I get a loan within 1 hour in Malaysia?
Only through credit card cash advance (if you have a card) or pre-approved loan app offers. All other options take at minimum several hours.

### What is the fastest loan app in Malaysia?
TNG GOpinjam and AEON iCash are among the fastest — but only for users who already have pre-approved offers. You cannot sign up and get a loan the same day.

### Can I get RM10,000 today?
Possible through licensed money lenders if you visit their office with complete documents in the morning. Digital lenders may also approve this amount for existing customers with good history.

### What's the cheapest fast loan option?
Family/friends (0%), followed by Ar-Rahnu (~2%/month), then bank personal loans (5-12% p.a.). Licensed money lenders (12-18% p.a.) and credit card cash advances (15-18% p.a.) are the most expensive.

### Do I need good credit for a fast loan?
Loan apps and banks — yes. Licensed money lenders — they check CCRIS but are more lenient. Ar-Rahnu/pawn shops — no credit check at all.

### Are "instant approval" loans legitimate?
Some are (like pre-approved bank offers and established loan apps). Many advertised "instant loans" online are scams. Always verify through official channels.

### Is it safe to borrow from licensed money lenders?
Yes, if they are genuinely licensed. Always verify at www.kpkt.gov.my, visit their physical office, and never pay upfront fees. They are regulated by law with maximum 18% p.a. interest.

### What should I do if I fell for a loan scam?
Report immediately to PDRM (03-2610 1222) and Bank Negara (1-300-88-5465). If you shared bank details, contact your bank to freeze the account.
