## Interest Rates (Historical)

Cashwagon and similar platforms typically charged:

| Fee Type | Typical Range |
|----------|---------------|
| Interest Rate | 0.5% - 1.5% per day |

<Callout type="danger" title="Extremely High Rates">
Daily interest rates like 1% per day sound small but equal 365% per year. These short-term loans are extremely expensive. A RM1,000 loan could cost RM300+ in just one month.
</Callout>

### Cost Reality Check

RM2,000 loan for 30 days at 1% daily:
- Daily interest: RM20
- 30-day interest: RM600
- Total repayment: RM2,600
- **Effective cost: 30% for one month**

This is why short-term loan apps should only be absolute last resort.

## The Debt Trap Warning

Short-term high-interest loans create debt spirals:

1. **Borrow RM1,000** — seems manageable
2. **Owe RM1,300** after 30 days — can't pay full
3. **Reborrow to pay** — now owe more
4. **Cycle continues** — debt grows exponentially
5. **End result** — paying thousands for original small loan

</Callout>

## How to Check If a Lender Is Legitimate

Before using ANY online lender:

1. **Search SSM** — Is the company registered in Malaysia?
2. **Check Bank Negara** — Are they licensed?
3. **Read reviews** — What do real users say?
4. **Calculate true cost** — Daily rate × 365 = annual rate
5. **Look for physical address** — Can you visit their office?
6. **Test customer service** — Do they respond professionally?
