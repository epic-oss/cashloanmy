## What is Fast Ringgit?

Fast Ringgit is a digital lending platform in Malaysia offering personal loans with quick approval times. It targets Malaysians who need fast access to cash without the lengthy bank application process.

**Quick Facts:**

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Loan Type | Personal loan |
| Loan Amount | RM1,000 - RM20,000 (varies) |
| Tenure | 3 - 12 months |
| Approval Time | Minutes to 24 hours |
| Platform | Mobile app |

## How Fast Ringgit Works

1. **Download the app** from Google Play or App Store
2. **Register** with IC and personal details
3. **Submit documents** (IC photo, selfie, bank statements)
4. **Get assessment** — algorithm evaluates your profile
5. **Receive offer** — loan amount and terms if approved
6. **Accept and receive funds** — disbursed to your bank account

## Is Fast Ringgit Legit?

**What to check for any loan app:**

1. **Licensed?** — Check if registered with Bank Negara or relevant authority
2. **Clear terms?** — Interest rates and fees should be transparent
3. **Physical presence?** — Legitimate companies have registered addresses
4. **Reviews?** — Check Google Play, App Store, and forums
5. **No upfront fees?** — Legitimate lenders never ask for payment before disbursement

<Callout type="danger" title="Red Flag">
If any loan app asks you to pay a "processing fee" or "insurance" before receiving your loan, it's likely a scam. Legitimate lenders deduct fees from the disbursed amount.
</Callout>

## How to Apply Safely

If you decide to use Fast Ringgit or any loan app:

1. **Download only from official stores** — Google Play or App Store
2. **Check app permissions** — Be wary of apps wanting access to contacts, photos, etc.
3. **Read all terms** — Know exactly what you're agreeing to
4. **Borrow only what you need** — Don't take maximum offered
5. **Have a repayment plan** — Missing payments gets expensive fast
6. **Screenshot everything** — Keep records of terms and payments
